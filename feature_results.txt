SELECTED: DEGREE + LOG DEGREE + BETWEENNESS CENTRALITY + ONE HOT
REASONS:
Degree
What it measures: Number of interactions a node has.

Biological meaning:
High-degree circRNAs or miRNAs interact with many partners
Indicates interaction richness or well-studied regulatory hubs

Explains:
How involved is this node in the biological network?
(It captures participation, not mechanism.)

Log-Degree
What it measures: Scaled version of degree to handle large hubs.

Biological meaning:
Prevents highly connected nodes from dominating learning
Allows moderately connected but meaningful nodes to contribute

Explains:
Is this node important beyond sheer popularity?

Betweenness Centrality
What it measures: Frequency of a node lying on shortest paths.

Biological meaning:
Identifies regulatory bridge nodes, especially miRNAs
Highlights nodes that mediate circRNA–miRNA–disease pathways

Explains:
Is this node critical for biological information flow?
(This provides path-based, mechanistic interpretability.)

Node-Type One-Hot
What it measures: Semantic role of the node (circRNA / miRNA / disease).

Biological meaning:
Distinguishes regulators, mediators, and outcomes
Prevents structurally similar nodes from being misinterpreted

Explains:
What biological role does this node play?
